Welcome to Dormir

Find the best room for you!

What is dormir? Find the dorm room perfect for you. Think Yelp, Instagram, and Red Fin, but for dorm rooms. Find rooms on campus with images and real talk from Wellesley students who have lived in those rooms. Each room review is assigned a dorm building, closest dining hall, and the average review rating. Rate, add pictures, comment your real thoughts on your dorm room experience. Using the search bar, you can search by dorm building or specific dorm hall. If no results show, it's because there isn't a review yet! It's super easy to use the platform, just log in with your Wellesley credentials and start searching, reviewing, and bookmarking rooms you like!
